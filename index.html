Become a paid BIGO host

Three steps to go live
with FZ Agency

No experience needed — just energy and a willingness to show up. Here's how you get started.

STEP 01

Download BIGO Live

Get the BIGO Live app on iOS or Android and set up your account. It's free.

STEP 02

Create your profile

Add your photo, a short bio, and your go-live basics so you're ready to shine on camera.

STEP 03

Apply to FZ Agency

Fill out the form below or message us on WhatsApp. We'll set you up with host status and get you started.

Then: orientation

Once you're in, we walk you through everything at orientation — how earnings actually work, from gifts (Beans) and hours streamed to incentives — plus real tips from hosts who've built their income here.

Two ways to apply — pick whatever's easier

Option A · Through us

Apply on this site

  1. Download BIGO Live and create your profile.
  2. Fill out the application form below — or WhatsApp us at +1 (917) 512-3583.
  3. We'll reach out and set up your host status.
Option B · In the app

Apply directly in BIGO Live

  1. Tap Me in the bottom-right corner.
  2. Open Creator Center.
  3. Tap Become a Broadcaster.
  4. Tap the magnifying glass, search FashZip, then tap Apply.
